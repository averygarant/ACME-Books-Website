About ACME Books

Our History

ACME Books was founded in 2013 in Grand Rapids, MI. We were founded on the hopes to provide the warmth that reading brings readers, to the shopping experience everyone must take to find a new book. ACME books prioritizes our customers and their experiences. Our goal is to bring the small town, local business feel to those across the country, and to offer more oppurtunities for Indie authors to have their books advertised. Every author works hard to publish and get their book into the hands of loving readers, and we are always looking to help this happen easier. Since our founding 10 years ago, we have helped over 130 independent authors increase their sales and become picked up by large publication comapanies and we plan to help so many more.

Our Mission

Our mission has always been to provide quality books to as many people as we can manage. In an age where technology is becoming every more present, we believe it is important to remember some of the simpler things. Many of the older generation can remember many lazy afternoons spent reading a good book, and our hope to allow for the creation of more of those memories, for those both young and old
